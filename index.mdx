## About MailBlock

Mailblock is a developer-first email delivery platform that makes sending transactional emails simple and reliable. With pre-verified domains and ready-to-use SDKs, you can start sending emails in minutes without complex DNS setup.

Mailblock provides built-in deliverability, analytics, and debugging tools so your messages reach the inbox with confidence.
